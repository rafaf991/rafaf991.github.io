  • RG flows, CFTs and defects (Boundaries: Topic of my master thesis (to be uploaded) project with Agnese. Lines: Project with Miguel (to be uploaded)),

  • Algebraic quantum field theory ( Notes on AQFT, the Unruh effect and measurements in quantum field theory here)
