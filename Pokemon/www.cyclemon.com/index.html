    My name is Rafael Felipe Cordoba Lopez. I am a physicist and matematician interested in quantum field theory and geometry.

    In particular, I am interested in the following topics:

    • topolopgical quantum field theory (TQFT),
    • algebraic quantum field theory (AQFT),
    • loop quantum gravity (LQG) and,
    • geometrical tools in string theory.

    Currently I am a MSc student in Physics at The Ludwig-Maximilians Universität (LMU) and a High energy diploma student at the International Center for Theoretical Physics (ICTP).
