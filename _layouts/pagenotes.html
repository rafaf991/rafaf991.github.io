Warning! This page is in english and spanish (and possibly in other langauges in the future)

My name is Rafael Felipe Cordoba Lopez. I am a physicist and matematician interested in gravitation and the foundations of quantum field theory. Specifically I am interested in foundational aspects of QFTs, CFTs, generalized symmetries and quantum gravity. Right now I have been exploring the following topics:

  • Quantum gravity and RG flows
  • CFTs and defects (Boundaries: Topic of my master thesis (to be uploaded) project with Agnese. Lines: Project with Miguel (to be uploaded)),
  • Topological quantum field theory (Knots and CS as a quantum gravity theory),
  • Algebraic quantum field theory ( Notes on AQFT, the Unruh effect and measurements in quantum field theory here)
Click here to find some lecture notes and pdfs on topics i have been atracted to.

Currently I am student of the École Normale Supérieure (ENS)– PSL in Paris, France. Click here to see what I been up to.
